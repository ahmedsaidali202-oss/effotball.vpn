Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before securing your connection.

What is a VPN?
A VPN (Virtual Private Network) creates an encrypted tunnel between your device and the internet. It protects your personal data from eavesdroppers and hides your IP address.
Why should gamers use a VPN?
Gamers use VPNs to protect their accounts against credential theft on public networks, avoid ISP throttling, safeguard against DDoS attacks, and mask their real geographical locations.
Is it easy to install?
Yes! You simply download the application on your phone or PC, log in, and tap the "Connect" button. It takes under two minutes from setup to full protection.
How does it protect privacy on public Wi-Fi?
Unsecured public Wi-Fi networks allow hackers to intercept data. A VPN encrypts all traffic leaving your mobile phone, making your passwords and PES login details unreadable to attackers.
